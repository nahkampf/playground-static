Playground Fanzine

Playground is a paper fanzine about larps. Read about larps that have been, larps that might be, and larps that never were.

The fanzine is focused on participants sharing their experiences from larps they've participated in, but anything larp related can find its way onto our pages if it's interesting!

All issues of Playground Fanzine are free to download in PDF format (see below). The hardcopies are printed in limited runs and are available upon request (contact us!).
